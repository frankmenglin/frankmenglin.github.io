133B

133B discussion 03312020
  • We review what is European Put&Call Option.
  • We make a few ideal assumptions about the market (no transaction cost, no bid-ask spread, can borrow/deposit at risk free rate).
  • We make the assumption that the market is efficient so no arbitrage profit can be made.
  • We use no arbitrage principle to make a few conclusions: put-call parity equation, monotonicity and convexity of Put&Call option price with respect to strike price.
